**Challenge 1**: What Is Generative AI?

- Task: Use PartyRock [https://partyrock.aws/](https://partyrock.aws/) to create
  a website that can explain the following.
- What is Generative AI?
- What are LLMs? (Include 2–3 examples like GPT, Claude, LLaMA)
- What are Transformers, and why are they important?
- Bonus: Share a meme, diagram, or short explainer video on you social media
  pages to teach others in your own style.

**Challenge 2**: Prompting Like a Pro

- Task: Use PartyRock to create a website that can explain
  [https://partyrock.aws/](https://partyrock.aws/) the difference between
  **Prompt Engineering** and **Context Engineering**.

Create examples of:

- Zero-shot prompting
- One-shot prompting
- Two-shot prompting

Bonus: Share a meme, diagram, or short explainer video and share on your social
media pages to teach others in your own style.

**Challenge 3**: Inside the AI Model's Brain

- Task: In your own words, explain:
- What are Neural Networks? (Use visuals if you like –
  [Optional Video Series](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLAqhIrjkxbuWI23v9cThsA9GvCAUhRvKZ)).

  real examples like GPT or LLaMA.

Bonus: Create a mini flowchart or sketch showing the training lifecycle of an
LLM and share on your social media pages.
